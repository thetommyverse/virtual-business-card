Tommy Hills

Performer / Comedian / Educator

Tommy is a passionate STEM communicator and experimentalist based in Milton Keynes, currently balancing roles as an MSc by Research student at the University of Kent, a Computing teacher with Teach First, and a PowerPoint comedian. Blending a love of stand-up comedy with work in artificial intelligence, Tommy delivers engaging talks that highlight the creativity within Computing. His mission is to inspire others by showing how AI, Mathematics, and Computer Science connect to everyday life beyond the classroom.
